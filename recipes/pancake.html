Pancake

Description

Fluffy, golden-brown pancakes stacked high and topped with a pat of
melting butter and a generous drizzle of maple syrup
â€” a warm, comforting breakfast classic perfect for any morning.

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. in another bowl, whisk milk, egg,and butter
  3. combine wt and dry, mix until just smooth
  4. heat pan, grease lightly
  5. pour batter, cook until bubbles form, flip cook other side
  6. serve with butter and syrup
